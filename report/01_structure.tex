\subsection{Our Assumptions}
We assume that if a replica crashes after receiving a write request from a client,the operation will time out from the client's perspective. 
 Nevertheless, the write operation will still be processed and applied by the coordinator and the remaining correct replicas following the election of a new coordinator.
Furthermore, we assume that a newly elected coordinator cannot crash during the synchronization phase (i.e., while broadcasting the \texttt{Synchronization} message)
